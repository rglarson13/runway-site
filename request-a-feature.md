Have an idea for Runway?

I'd love to hear what would make Runway more useful for you. Whether it's a new feature, a workflow improvement, or something that would make your financial planning easier, your feedback helps shape future versions.

## Before you suggest something

Runway is intentionally built around a few principles:

- No required accounts, no cloud sync, no central servers, etc
- No selling or sharing of your financial data
- No unnecessary complexity
- Cash-flow forecasting over traditional budgeting

Some ideas may not fit those principles, but I read every suggestion.

## What to include

The most helpful requests include:

- What you'd like Runway to do
- Why it would be useful to you
- How you currently handle this today (if applicable)
